Livelihood

Skill Development & Training

Empowering youth and workers through vocational training programs that enhance employability and technical proficiency across sectors like manufacturing, electronics, and automotive.

MSME Support & Innovation

Encouraging Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises with infrastructure, funding assistance, and digitalization to boost production, competitiveness, and job creation.

Industrial Cluster Promotion

Creating well-equipped industrial clusters to support startups and medium-scale industries, improve logistics, and enhance collaboration within supply chains.

Green & Sustainable Manufacturing

Promoting eco-friendly production techniques, energy-efficient machinery, and sustainable waste management practices for a greener industrial future.

Women Empowerment Initiatives

Supporting women-led SHGs by providing micro-loans, entrepreneurial training, and marketing platforms to foster financial independence and community leadership.

Digital & Financial Literacy
